TOURIST CENTERS IN ORO

National Museum Oron

Oron Museum is a museum near Calabar, Nigeria.
The museum was established in 1958 to accommodate eight hundred known ancestral figures (ekpu) of the Oron people which are believed to be among the oldest and finest surviving wood carvings in Africa
